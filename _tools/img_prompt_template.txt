===============================================================================
  imagesprompts.txt FORMAT
===============================================================================

Each image = 2 blocks separated by a blank line:

  ORDER NUMBER : EXACT SCRIPT TEXT

  Hand-drawn 2D stick figure animation style, flat white background.
  [scene description - subject, action, minimal environment, centered]
  Black outlines, flat fills, no shading, no gradients.

  (blank line before next image)

EXAMPLE:

1: Farming is a mistake.

Hand-drawn 2D stick figure animation style, flat white background.
A stick figure stands shocked, hands raised, text "A MISTAKE" above.
Black outlines, flat fills, no shading, no gradients.

2: For 300,000 years ancestors walked past wild wheat.

Hand-drawn 2D stick figure animation style, flat white background.
A stick figure walks past wheat stalks, sun with rays in corner.
Black outlines, flat fills, no shading, no gradients.
